p. Aequor Model

Summary

In this project, I created an factory function that creates models of a fictional organism called p. Aequor. Each object has a strand of randomly generated nucleotides, a method to compare what percent of the DNA is identical to another object's DNA, and a method to determine if it's likely to survive based on given criteria about the nucleotides.
